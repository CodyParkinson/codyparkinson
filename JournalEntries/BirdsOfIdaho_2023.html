Trumpeter Swan - Beaver Dick Park - 04/10/2023




This is one of my projects for the year. Although I am starting here in mid-march, my goal is to identify as many Idaho birds as I possibly can.

The list of Idaho birds I am using comes from Birds of Idaho Field Guide by Stan Tekiela. I have looked through quite a few books, and I have found this one to be the most comprehensive - without including some of the more 'rare' species. Don't get me wrong there are a lot of birds in this book that might take a large amount of effort to find, but Stan Tekiela doesn't include birds that are only seen every few years or ones that have only been spotted on the border like one time. The birds listed are ones that are truly 'native to Idaho'. I have come across some birds that are not included in the book, and I have added those to the list as I find them. I'll take any extras as an extra win!

With that, I am very happy with this list, and I can't wait to get to birding!
