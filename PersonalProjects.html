Feet Up

The road to climbing 1,000,000 feet. This is an idea I had while reading about Christopher Fisher’s Max Vert October 2021. He managed to climb over 400k feet in a single month, and when you read about it, you can’t help but feel inspired to climb. I am nowhere near Mr.Fisher’s level of endurance or athleticism, so I will be doing my elevation over the course of 2 years. There are a lot of peaks I am looking to bag, so see y'all on the mountain.
