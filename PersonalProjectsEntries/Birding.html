What even is this?

I don’t know what it is, but I just kind of like birds. There really is no rhyme or reason to it, and I have no idea how I even got into it. But, I just have a soft spot for these dinosaur-esque creatures.

Really, the only goal is to document as many different species as possible. I am not sure if I have enough memory or space to put every single picture of every single bird I see, but I will update bird species with the best photo I have of them. I will also try to keep my Google Sheet updated with sightings and locations.
